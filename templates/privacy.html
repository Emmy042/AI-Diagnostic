Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the AI Dermatology Diagnostic Support prototype. As an academic project, we prioritize the privacy and security of all users interacting with this system.

2. Data Collection & Processing

This prototype is designed with a strict "no-storage" policy. Images uploaded to the system are processed in-memory for the sole purpose of generating a diagnostic prediction and are immediately discarded. We do not store, log, or persist any medical images, user IP addresses, or patient health information (PHI).

3. Third-Party Services

This system operates locally or within an isolated academic hosting environment. It does not integrate with third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services.

4. Educational Nature

The data processed by this system is intended strictly for academic demonstration and research evaluation. It is not designed to comply with comprehensive healthcare data regulations (such as HIPAA or GDPR) because no data is retained.

5. Changes to Policy

As this is an ongoing academic project, this policy may be updated to reflect changes in the prototype's architecture. Any changes will be documented here.
